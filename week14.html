Lottie

  • I've learnt what are Lottie, how to use them, and the tools & resources available for them.

  • Lottie is a extremely lightweight animation & design, because it's just code.

  • Lottie is a great way to perform animations and allow additional interactivity on your site or mobile platforms.

  • They are JSON-based animation file format that enables designers to ship animations on any platform as easily as shipping static.

  • They are small files that work on any device and can scale up or down without pixelation.

  • Lottie was first created by AirBnB: https://airbnb.design/lottie
